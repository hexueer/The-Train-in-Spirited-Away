Creative Scene

created by Yihui Liao and Ivy Ho

On this page, we have begun to build out a scene from the animated film Spirited Away which was directed by Hayao Miyazaki. This is the scene where a train that travels is arriving at a "bus stop" in the middle of a body of water to bring the main character to her destination.

We have built out the main components of the train's exterior and interior, implemented water using a plane mapped with mirror-like material, and also built the train tracks (made from box geometries) on top of a rock path (texture mapped).

In particular, the train is a fairly complex object in our scene since we used a Three CSG (constructive solid geometry) library to transform and merge multiple geometries, which gave us wonderful features like windows that are actually cut out of the train.

The animation of the train has also been implemented so that it slows down, stops at the platform, the doors proceed to open and close, and then slowly moves again. This is repeated in a continuous cycle.

Users can change the camera perspective by pressing the 'a' key to view the train from the side, the 's' key to follow the train as it moves, the 'd' key to stand at the platform, and the 'f' key to sit inside the train.

Another fantastic feature is the audio (the famous score from Spirited Away) that plays immediately upon loading the page.
